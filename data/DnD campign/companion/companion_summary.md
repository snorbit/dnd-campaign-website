Companion Summary

Name: Lyrin Valehart
Role: Companion NPC / Secret Traitor
Public Description: A polite, soft‑spoken mage who assists the party and gains their trust.

Betrayal Event: Reveals allegiance during the final confrontation with Ainz and turns against the party (unless flipped).
