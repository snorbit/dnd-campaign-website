# World Items - Swords (154-170)

## 154. Abyssal Sword
- **Type**: World Item (Sword)
- **Effect**: Channels abyssal power
- **Ability**: Can corrupt any target
- **Special**: Cannot be purified
- **D&D 5e**: Abyssal power + corruption
- **Appearance**: Black sword with demonic runes that glow red
- **History**: Forged in the deepest abyss by demon lords
- **Attunement**: Must accept demonic corruption
- **Powers**:
  - Deals extra necrotic damage that can't be healed
  - Corrupts good-aligned creatures on hit
  - Can cut through any magical protection
  - Summons demons when drawn
- **Damage**: 4d8 slashing + 4d6 necrotic + corruption effect
- **Curse**: Wearer slowly becomes demon-aligned
- **Limitations**: Cannot harm demons or abyssal creatures
