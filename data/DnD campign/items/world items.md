# World Items Without Holders

## Unassigned World Items

### Atlas (41)
- **Type**: World Item
- **Effect**: Creates and manipulates pocket dimensions
- **Ability**: Can store up to 1000 cubic feet of matter in a dimensional space
- **Special**: Once stolen from Nine's Own Goal, now lost in the world
- **Usage**: Requires attunement, can be used 3 times per day
- **D&D 5e**: Functions as a bag of holding that can also trap creatures inside

### Billion Blades (42)
- **Type**: World Item (Weapon)
- **Effect**: Manifests infinite blades of pure energy
- **Ability**: Can create 1000 spectral blades that attack all enemies in 100ft radius
- **Damage**: Each blade deals 4d6 force damage, no save allowed
- **Special**: Once used, blades disappear and must be recreated
- **D&D 5e**: Area of effect weapon that ignores armor and cover

### Hygieia's Chalice (43)
- **Type**: World Item (Chalice)
- **Effect**: Purifies and enhances all forms of healing
- **Ability**: Can cure any disease, poison, or curse instantly
- **Healing**: Can heal up to 500 HP per day, can resurrect without penalty
- **Special**: Water from the chalice can heal multiple allies simultaneously
- **D&D 5e**: Greater restoration + mass cure wounds combined

## World Item Acquisition Rules

**Discovery Methods:**
- Ancient ruins and dungeons
- Divine intervention
- Defeating powerful World Item holders
- Completing impossible quests
- Random dimensional anomalies

**Attunement Requirements:**
- Must be level 20 or higher
- Must complete special quest or trial
- Must sacrifice something of equal value
- Must be deemed worthy by the item itself

**Usage Limitations:**
- Most World Items require attunement
- Many have daily or weekly usage limits
- Some require specific conditions or locations
- All attract powerful enemies when used

**Balance Notes:**
- World Items should be campaign-defining artifacts
- Consider limiting player access to maintain game balance
- Each World Item should have major story significance
- World Items attract divine and cosmic attention
