# World Items - Crowns (134-153)

## 134. Abyssal Crown
- **Type**: World Item (Crown)
- **Effect**: Rules over abyss
- **Ability**: Can command demonic forces
- **Special**: Cannot be removed by force
- **D&D 5e**: Abyss control + demon command
- **Appearance**: Black crown with glowing red runes that pulse with demonic energy
- **History**: Forged in the deepest pits of the abyss, worn by demon lords
- **Attunement**: Requires making a pact with a powerful demon
- **Powers**: 
  - Command up to 100 HD of demons
  - Create portals to abyssal realms
  - Resist all holy damage
  - Speak abyssal fluently
- **Limitations**: Cannot be used by good-aligned creatures
- **Curse**: Slowly corrupts wearer toward evil alignment
