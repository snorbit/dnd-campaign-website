# World Items - Armor (188-200)

## 188. Abyssal Armor
- **Type**: World Item (Armor)
- **Effect**: Protects with abyssal power
- **Ability**: Can resist any attack
- **Special**: Cannot be removed
- **D&D 5e**: Abyssal protection + immovable defense
- **Appearance**: Black plate armor with demonic runes and spikes
- **History**: Forged in the deepest abyss from demon souls
- **Attunement**: Must accept demonic corruption
- **Armor Class**: 25 + Dexterity modifier (max 2)
- **Powers**:
  - Resistance to all damage types
  - Cannot be removed against wearer's will
  - Regenerates damage quickly
  - Intimidates all who see it
- **Immunities**: Poison, necrotic, psychic damage
- **Curse**: Wearer slowly becomes demon-aligned
- **Special**: Cannot be damaged by any means
