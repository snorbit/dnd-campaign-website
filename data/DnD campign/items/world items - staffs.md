# World Items - Staffs (171-187)

## 171. Abyssal Staff
- **Type**: World Item (Staff)
- **Effect**: Channels abyssal power
- **Ability**: Can summon abyssal creatures
- **Special**: Cannot be purified
- **D&D 5e**: Abyssal power + creature summoning
- **Appearance**: Black staff topped with screaming skull
- **History**: Carved from abyssal rock and demon bones
- **Attunement**: Must make pact with powerful demon
- **Powers**:
  - Summon up to 100 HD of demons
  - Cast abyssal spells at will
  - Create portals to abyssal realms
  - Resist all holy magic
- **Spells**: Can cast any abyssal or demonic spell
- **Curse**: Wearer slowly becomes demon-aligned
- **Limitations**: Cannot be used by good-aligned creatures
