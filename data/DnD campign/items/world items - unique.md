# World Items - Unique & Miscellaneous (1-60, 61-133)

## The Twenty (1-20) - Most Powerful World Items

### 1. The Crown of the First King
- **Type**: World Item (One of The Twenty)
- **Effect**: Absolute Hierarchy
- **Ability**: All sentient beings within the wielder's domain must obey lawful commands or suffer psychic damage equal to their max HP.
- **Special**: Cannot command other World Item holders. But can command armies instantly.
- **D&D 5e**: *Geas* (No save) on a mass scale. Charisma becomes 30.

### 2. Scepter of the Void
- **Type**: World Item (One of The Twenty)
- **Effect**: Erasure Command
- **Ability**: Pointing the scepter deletes matter in a 10ft cube.
- **Special**: Can erase magical barriers, walls of force, and even portions of the ground (creating pits to the void).
- **D&D 5e**: *Disintegrate* (at 9th level) at will.

### 3. Chalice of Eternity
- **Type**: World Item (One of The Twenty)
- **Effect**: True Immortality
- **Ability**: Drinking from the chalice stops aging and grants regeneration that defies death.
- **Special**: The user cannot die from age, disease, or poison.
- **D&D 5e**: Regain 20 HP at start of every turn. Immune to exhaustion.

### 4. Hammer of Creation
- **Type**: World Item (One of The Twenty)
- **Effect**: World Forging
- **Ability**: Striking the ground creates structures, terrain, or items instantly from raw mana.
- **Special**: Can repair any item, even other World Items.
- **D&D 5e**: Cast *Fabricate* and *Move Earth* at will. 1/Day *Wish* (to create object).

### 5. Lens of Truth
- **Type**: World Item (One of The Twenty)
- **Effect**: Absolute Perception
- **Ability**: Sees through all illusions, shapechanging, darkness, and lies.
- **Special**: Perceives the stat blocks of enemies.
- **D&D 5e**: Truesight 120ft. Immune to Blindness. Passive Perception 30.

### 6. Key of Doors
- **Type**: World Item (One of The Twenty)
- **Effect**: Universal Access
- **Ability**: Opens any lock, seal, or portal (including planar boundaries).
- **D&D 5e**: *Knock* at will. Cast *Gate* 1/day without components.

### 7. Ignis Heart
- **Type**: World Item (One of The Twenty)

### 134. The Micro-Transaction Blade (P.T.W.)
- **Type**: Unique World Item (Sword)
- **Effect**: Gold-to-Damage Conversion
- **Ability**: The "Pay-to-Win" mechanic.
- **Base Damage**: 0.
- **Micro-transaction**: As a free action, the wielder can feed Gold into the pommel. Every 1 GP spent adds +1 Force damage to the blade's "Damage Pool."
- **The Subscription Model**:
    - **Standard Account**: If the lifetime Gold spent on the sword is **less than 5,000 GP**, the damage pool resets at the end of the **ROUND**.
    - **Premium Member (Penalty)**: If the lifetime Gold spent on the sword is **5,000 GP or more**, the software "updates" to a less efficient model. The damage pool now resets at the end of every **TURN**.
- **Special**: 
    - The sword glows with RGB lighting that changes color based on how much gold is currently in the pool.
    - It emits a "Cha-Ching" sound effect on every crit.
- **D&D 5e**: Scalable damage + economy-based scaling.
- **Appearance**: A sleek, futuristic-looking sword with a digital display and a coin slot.
- **History**: Rumored to have been forged by a god of greed who loved mobile gaming.
