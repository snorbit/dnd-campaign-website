# Notable NPCs (Non-Nazarick)

## Imperial High Mage Veldris
- **Affiliation**: Re-Founded Empire
- **Role**: Head of Arcane Regulation
- **Personality**: Pragmatic but paranoid
- **Appearance**: Elderly human with piercing blue eyes and silver-threaded robes
- **Hooks**:
  - Seeks "reliable" mages for special projects
  - Has information about ancient magical wards
  - Suspects a traitor in the Imperial Court
  - Knows of a forgotten imperial vault containing dangerous artifacts

## Syndicate Master Thalric
- **Affiliation**: Adventurers' Syndicate (Neutral Faction)
- **Role**: Rogue turned crime lord
- **Personality**: Charismatic but ruthless
- **Appearance**: Middle-aged half-elf with numerous scars and expensive but practical clothing
- **Hooks**:
  - Knows secret routes through the city
  - Has eyes and ears everywhere
  - Will trade favors for protection
  - Looking for someone to eliminate a rival without it being traced back to him

## Sister Maris of the Silent Choir
- **Affiliation**: The Silent Choir
- **Role**: Zealot Leader
- **Personality**: Fanatical, uncompromising
- **Appearance**: Middle-aged human woman with shaved head and simple gray robes
- **Hooks**:
  - Believes she can "cure" magic users
  - Has a personal vendetta against Nazarick
  - Knows ancient anti-magic rituals
  - Seeks a legendary artifact that can nullify magic in a large area

## Merchant Lord Elian
- **Affiliation**: Free Cities Compact
- **Role**: Wealthy Merchant Prince
- **Personality**: Pragmatic, profit-driven, but with a code of honor
- **Appearance**: Well-dressed human in his 40s with a neatly trimmed beard and calculating eyes
- **Hooks**:
  - Funds expeditions to recover lost artifacts
  - Has connections in every major city
  - Willing to bend rules for the right price
  - Has a daughter who has gone missing under mysterious circumstances

## Captain Rennick
- **Affiliation**: Free Cities Compact
- **Role**: City Guard Captain
- **Personality**: Grizzled but fair, values justice over politics
- **Appearance**: Battle-scarred human with a military bearing and a permanent scowl
- **Hooks**:
  - Investigating a series of mysterious disappearances
  - Knows the city's underworld better than most
  - Willing to look the other way for the right cause

## Liora the Fence
- **Affiliation**: Independent (Formerly Adventurers' Syndicate)
- **Role**: Information Broker
- **Personality**: Witty, resourceful, always has an angle
- **Appearance**: Young halfling woman with quick hands and quicker wit
- **Hooks**:
  - Knows where to find anything... for a price
  - Has dirt on nearly everyone important
  - Looking for protection from a former employer

## Brother Tomas
- **Affiliation**: Temple of the Dawnfather
- **Role**: Healer and Scholar
- **Personality**: Kind-hearted but naive, believes in the good of all people
- **Appearance**: Middle-aged human with kind eyes and simple priestly robes
- **Hooks**:
  - Runs an underground railroad for magic users
  - Has discovered a disturbing prophecy
  - Needs help protecting refugees from the Silent Choir

## Zorvak the Unseen
- **Affiliation**: None (Mercenary)
- **Role**: Master Assassin
- **Personality**: Cold, professional, never breaks a contract
- **Appearance**: Human male of indeterminate age, easily forgettable features
- **Hooks**:
  - For the right price, anyone can disappear
  - Knows the location of several hidden safe houses
  - Has a personal code that sometimes conflicts with his contracts
