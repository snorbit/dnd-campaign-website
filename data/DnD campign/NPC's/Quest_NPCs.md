# Quest NPCs

## Session 1: Oakhaven Village (Act 1-3)

### Mayor Eldrin <a name="mayor-eldrin"></a>
- **Location**: Village Square (Oakhaven)
- **Role**: Town Mayor / Administrative Head
- **Personality**: Nervous, duty-bound, currently overwhelmed by guilt and fear.
- **Quests**:
  - **"The Oakhaven Rescue"**: Find and rescue the kidnapped villagers, especially Elara.
- **Rewards**: 50gp per player, official "Oakhaven Hero" status.

### Garrick Ironhand <a name="garrick-ironhand"></a>
- **Location**: Ironhand Forge (Oakhaven)
- **Role**: Master Smith / Devastated Father
- **Personality**: Stoic but boiling with rage; his only focus is his daughter Elara.
- **Quests**:
  - **"Ironhand's Revenge"**: Find Elara and deliver a message of vengeance to the Red Eye leader.
- **Rewards**: Free basic gear, "Blacksmith's Reserve" whetstones, and future crafting discounts.

### Lila the Baker <a name="lila-the-baker"></a>
- **Location**: Lila's Bakery (Oakhaven)
- **Role**: Local Baker / Heartbroken Partner
- **Personality**: Kind-hearted, currently traumatized by the raid.
- **Quests**:
  - **"The Guard's Wake"**: Help find a way to wake Captain Horgus from his magical slumber.
- **Rewards**: Rations, information on village secrets.

## Quest Givers

### Lord Edric Duskveil
- **Location**: The Obsidian Spire (Re-Founded Empire)
- **Role**: Imperial Spymaster
- **Personality**: Calculating, discreet, always three steps ahead
- **Quests**:
  - "The Missing Artifact" - Recover a stolen imperial relic
  - "Eyes in the Dark" - Infiltrate a rival faction's stronghold
  - "The Traitor's Trail" - Uncover a mole in the imperial court
- **Rewards**: Gold, political favors, rare information

### Mistress Elara
- **Location**: The Crimson Coin Tavern (Adventurers' Syndicate)
- **Role**: Fence and Information Broker
- **Personality**: Charismatic, always has an angle, knows everyone's secrets
- **Quests**:
  - "The Black Market Auction" - Recover a dangerous magical item
  - "Debts to Pay" - Collect from dangerous clients
  - "The Silent Courier" - Deliver a mysterious package
- **Rewards**: Rare items, contacts, blackmail material

## Quest Targets

### The Bloody Baron
- **Location**: Blackwater (Free Cities Compact)
- **Role**: Crime Lord
- **Personality**: Ruthless, paranoid, always has an escape plan
- **Involvement**:
  - Wanted for numerous crimes
  - Holds valuable information about the city's underworld
  - Has connections to the Silent Choir
- **Defenses**: Heavily guarded mansion, numerous escape routes, loyal lieutenants

### Archmage Veylin
- **Location**: The Arcane Colleges (Re-Founded Empire)
- **Role**: Renowned Scholar
- **Personality**: Eccentric, brilliant, obsessed with knowledge
- **Involvement**:
  - Needs rare magical components
  - Researching forbidden magic
  - Being targeted by the Silent Choir
- **Location**: Tower of Whispers (heavily warded)

## Quest Allies

### Bram Thunderstone <a name="bram-thunderstone"></a>
- **Location**: The Rusty Flagon (Oakhaven)
- **Role**: Tavern Owner / Retired Mercenary
- **Personality**: Gruff, no-nonsense, values strength and a good drink.

  - Tactical advice on goblin behavior.
  - Information on "The Auditor" mark.

  - **"The Auditor's Trail"**: Investigate the strange iron marks on the raiders' armor.

### Mother Mathilda <a name="mother-mathilda"></a>
- **Location**: Chapel of the Morning Sun (Oakhaven)
- **Role**: Village Priestess / Secret Warlock
- **Personality**: Eerie, prophetic, speaks in riddles about "Old Things."

  - Healing and stabilization.

- **Quests**:
  - **"The Rot in the Woods"**: Investigate the unnatural decay seeping from the Whispering Woods.

## Quest Allies

### Captain Rennick
- **Location**: Various (Travelling)
- **Role**: Veteran Mercenary
- **Personality**: Grizzled but honorable, values loyalty
- **Assistance**:
  - Can provide backup in combat
  - Knows the local terrain and factions
  - Has a network of informants
- **Requirements**: Gold, help with personal quests, protection for his family

### Sister Elindra
- **Location**: Temple of the Dawnfather
- **Role**: High Priestess
- **Personality**: Compassionate but determined, believes in redemption
- **Assistance**:
  - Healing and restoration services
  - Information about ancient prophecies
  - Safe haven from certain factions
- **Needs Help With**:
  - Protecting refugees
  - Recovering stolen relics
  - Countering the Silent Choir's influence

### Korgath "Red Eye" <a name="korgath-red-eye"></a>
- **Location**: The Iron Lair (Whispering Woods)
- **Role**: Captain of the 7th Audit Platoon
- **Personality**: Cold, mechanical, views life as data points to be optimized.
- **Threats**:
  - Leading the "Red Eye" black site operations.
  - Wields "The Auditor" (Greatsword).
  - Possesses a magical ruby eye that hums with energy.
- **Vulnerabilities**: Overreliance on "logic" and "data"; becomes unstable when faced with unpredictable variables.

## Quest Hooks (Organized by Type)

### Mystery Quests
1. **The Vanishing Village**
   - **Key NPC**: Elder Brom (missing village leader)
   - **Hook**: Entire village disappeared overnight, only a strange symbol remains
   - **Twist**: The villagers willingly left to join a secret society

2. **The Cursed Artifact**
   - **Key NPC**: Relic Hunter Jessa
   - **Hook**: An ancient artifact is causing nightmares in a nearby town
   - **Twist**: The artifact is sentient and trying to warn about a greater threat

### Combat Quests
1. **The Bandit King**
   - **Key NPC**: Captain Rennick (ally), The Red Blade (enemy)
   - **Hook**: Bandit raids are increasing in frequency and organization
   - **Goal**: Take down the Bandit King and his lieutenants

2. **The Siege of Blackwater**
   - **Key NPC**: Merchant Lord Elian, The Bloody Baron
   - **Hook**: The city is under attack by an unknown force
   - **Goal**: Defend the city or exploit the chaos

### Political Quests
1. **The Peace Summit**
   - **Key NPC**: Lord Duskveil, Mistress Elara
   - **Hook**: Rival factions are meeting to discuss a fragile peace
   - **Goal**: Ensure the summit's success (or failure)

2. **The Missing Heir**
   - **Key NPC**: Various faction leaders
   - **Hook**: The heir to the throne has disappeared
   - **Goal**: Find the heir and navigate the political fallout

### Personal Quests
1. **Atonement**
   - **Key NPC**: Sister Elindra, The Faceless One
   - **Hook**: A party member's past comes back to haunt them
   - **Goal**: Make amends or embrace the past

2. **The Family Secret**
   - **Key NPC**: Various, depending on character backstory
   - **Hook**: Discover a shocking family secret
   - **Goal**: Uncover the truth and decide what to do with it
