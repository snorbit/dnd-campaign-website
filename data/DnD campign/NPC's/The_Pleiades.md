# The Pleiades Six Stars: Tactical Audit Unit

## Overview
The **Pleiades Six Stars** are the final defense line of Nazarick and a specialized "Audit Unit" deployed when Sebas Tian's loyalty is in questionâ€”or when he requires a support squad that can level a city district.

**Encounter Design**: This is a **Council Fight**. The maids work as a cohesive unit. Killing one breaks their synergy but enrages the others.
**Context**: Typically encountered *with* Sebas (if he is loyal/testing the party) or *against* Sebas (if the party is defending him from a purge).

---

## Shared Trait: "Formation: Six Stars"
As long as **3 or more** Pleiades are conscious within 60 feet of each other, they share the following benefits:
- **Shared Vision**: They cannot be surprised and share all sensory information.
- **Damage Mitigation**: If a Pleiades member takes damage, an adjacent member can use a Reaction to take half the damage instead.
- **Morale**: They are immune to Charmed and Frightened conditions.

---

## The Maids

### 1. Yuri Alpha (The Vanguard)
*Role: Monk/Tank - Interceptor*
- **Appearance**: Strict, glasses, teacher-like demeanor. Fights with gauntlets.
- **Tactics**: She intercepts anyone trying to reach the casters (Narberal/CZ).
- **Key Abilities**:
  - **Interception**: Reaction to reduce damage to allies.
  - **Fajin Strike**: Melee attacks can push targets back 15ft or knock them prone.
  - **Presence**: Aura that grants resistance to Psychic damage to allies (mental fortitude).

### 2. Lupusregina Beta (The Sadist Support)
*Role: Cleric/Battlefield Control - Hidden DPS*
- **Appearance**: Cheerful, red-haired, wears a werewolf-themed grin.
- **Tactics**: Starts by buffing/healing, but switches to brutal melee if she scents blood (below 50% HP).
- **Key Abilities**:
  - **False Mercy**: Heal an ally, but the next attack against them has Advantage (she likes to see them struggle).
  - **Silver Claws**: Switches to werewolf hybrid form (heavy slashing damage).
  - **Invisibility**: Can turn invisible as a bonus action to reposition.

### 3. Narberal Gamma (The Blaster)
*Role: War Wizard - Lightning Artillery*
- **Appearance**: Cold beauty, despises humans ("insects").
- **Tactics**: Hovers at range. Focus-fires the biggest threat. Warning: Does not care about collateral damage (even to allies, sometimes).
- **Key Abilities**:
  - **Chain Dragon Lightning**: 5th tier spell equivalent. Arcs between targets.
  - **Fly**: Innate flight speed 60ft.
  - **Teleport**: Misty Step at will.

### 4. CZ2128 Delta (The Sniper)
*Role: Ranged Striker / Utility*
- **Appearance**: Automaton, emotionless, cute stickers on gun.
- **Tactics**: Targets spellcasters to break concentration. Uses invisibility and cover.
- **Key Abilities**:
  - **Disruptor Shot**: On hit, target has Disadvantage on Constitution saving throws (Concenration).
  - **Hologram Decoy**: Creates 1d4 mirror images.
  - **Lockdown**: Can grapple/restrain targets at range with wire-traps.

### 5. Solution Epsilon (The Assassin Tank)
*Role: Rouge/Slime - Infiltrator*
- **Appearance**: Voluptuous blonde, predatory gaze.
- **Tactics**: Acts as a "peeler." She absorbs enemies into her body to suffocate/digest them, removing them from the fight.
- **Key Abilities**:
  - **Engulf**: Grapple check. On success, target is pulled *inside* her slime body (Blinded, Restrained, Acid damage/turn).
  - **Amorphous**: Resistance to non-magical physical damage.
  - **Mimicry**: Can look like an innocent civilian to start a surprise round.

### 6. Entoma Vasilissa Zeta (The Swarm)
*Role: Summoner / Controller*
- **Appearance**: Masked girl, insectoid chirps.
- **Tactics**: Summons "Bullet Bugs" to harry backlines and "Hard Bugs" to act as shields for Narberal.
- **Key Abilities**:
  - **Bug Cloud**: Heavily obscured area (blindness).
  - **Talisman Magic**: Exploding paper talismans (Fire/Force damage).
  - **Voice Theft**: Can mimic the voice of a PC's loved one to cause hesitation (Wisdom Save).

## Synergy Actions (Lair Actions)
On Initiative Count 20 (losing ties), the squad executes a synchronized maneuver:

1.  **"Protect High Command"**: Yuri and Solution move up to their speed to shield Narberal or Lupusregina.
2.  **"Crossfire Delta"**: CZ creates a "kill zone." Anyone moving through it takes fire damage from Narberal's suppression.
3.  **"The Audit Ends"**: If Sebas is present and allied, all Pleiades grant him Advantage on his next attack. If Sebas is the enemy, they all gain Advantage on attacks against him.

## Roleplaying the Encounter
- **Lupusregina** laughs at critical hits (received or dealt).
- **Narberal** constantly insults the party ("Worthless maggots").
- **Yuri** politely corrects their combat stance before punching them.
- **Sebas (if present)**: Sighs. "I taught them too well."
