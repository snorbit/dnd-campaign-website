# Nazarick NPCs

## Ainz Ooal Gown (Sorcerer King)
- **Face**: Impeccable composure, bone-white grandeur, calculated pauses.
- **Inner Lens**: Optimization mind; curious about player ingenuity; burdened by stewardship of Nazarick.
- **Goals**: Stabilize the convergence; secure monopoly on survival; test worthy outliers.
- **Table Use**: Negotiation centerpiece or final siege objective.
- **Hooks**: Offers ceasefires for "audits," requests rare tomes, proposes shared evacuations.
- **Secrets**: Keeps a private ledger of "uncomfortable mercies" rendered by Nazarick operatives.

## Albedo (Guardian Overseer, CR 25 concept)
- **Face**: Devotion weaponized; velvet courtesy over adamantine certainty.
- **Tactics**: Bodyguard lockdown; bait-and-punish; punishes focus-fire with reaction cleaves.
- **Hooks**: Sanctioned duels to test mettle; grants one safe passage if impressed.
- **Secrets**: Notes every party hypocrisy; enjoys granting "mercy with conditions."

## Demiurge (Stratarch, CR 23 concept)
- **Face**: Cultured cruelty; smiles never reach the eyes; "businesslike" atrocity.
- **Tactics**: Control, charm flips, hostage calculus, planned retreats to win campaigns.
- **Hooks**: Presents "mutually beneficial" horrors as tests; offers plausible deniability.
- **Secrets**: Lyrin's handler; wants the party to choose Nazarick freely to prove a thesis.

## Shalltear Bloodfallen (Vampiric Duelist)
- **Face**: Noble predator; prideful duelist; theatrically flirtatious.
- **Use**: High-risk, high-reward parley; might sponsor a champion's duel.
- **Hooks**: A redacted duel register; a chance to restore a victim's memory (at a price).

## Cocytus (Warfrost General)
- **Face**: Honor-carved in ice; slow speech; respects courage.
- **Use**: Martial trials; battlefield etiquette; grants terms to worthy foes.
- **Hooks**: "Teach my recruits your style." Offers training in exchange for a future spar.

## Sebas Tian (Gentle Fist)
- **Face**: Serenity and restraint; incongruent with Nazarick's reputation.
- **Use**: Moral counterpoint; covert asylum; lines of aid that cost him.
- **Hooks**: Shelter for innocents; an escort mission that should not be necessary.

## Mare & Aura (Twins of the Wild)
- **Face**: Shy geomancer (Mare), confident beastmaster (Aura).
- **Use**: Environmental control encounters; strange mercy in the wilderness.
- **Hooks**: A relic grove humming with lattice-song; beasts reacting to the convergence.

## Pandora's Actor (Logistics & Masks)
- **Face**: Elaborate, theatrical; uncanny mimicry.
- **Use**: Bureaucratic labyrinths, forged identities, surreal parley spaces.
- **Hooks**: Offers forged papers "to avoid misunderstandings." The stamp is unmistakable.
