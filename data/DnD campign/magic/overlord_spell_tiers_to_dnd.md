## Spell Power Rankings by Tier

### 0th Tier (Cantrips)
**Power Level**: Basic utility
**Caster Level**: 1st+
**Examples**:
- Light - Creates light source
- Mage Hand - Telekinetic manipulation
- Guidance - Divine assistance
- Prestidigitation - Minor magical effects
- Toll the Dead - Basic necrotic damage

### 1st Tier (1st Level)
**Power Level**: Minor combat/utility
**Caster Level**: 1st-3rd
**Examples**:
- Magic Missile - Force damage (auto-hit)
- Cure Wounds - Basic healing
- Shield - Defensive boost
- Detect Magic - Magical detection
- Bless - Combat enhancement

### 2nd Tier (2nd Level)
**Power Level**: Moderate effects
**Caster Level**: 3rd-5th
**Examples**:
- Fireball - Area damage (8d6)
- Invisibility - Stealth utility
- Mirror Image - Defensive illusions
- See Invisibility - Counter to invisibility
- Silence - Area spell suppression

### 3rd Tier (3rd Level)
**Power Level**: Significant power
**Caster Level**: 5th-7th
**Examples**:
- Counterspell - Magic negation
- Fly - Personal flight
- Lightning Bolt - Line damage (8d6)
- Revivify - Immediate resurrection
- Water Walk - Environmental adaptation

### 4th Tier (4th Level)
**Power Level**: Major combat magic
**Caster Level**: 7th-9th
**Examples**:
- Polymorph - Transformation
- Dimension Door - Teleportation (500 ft)
- Greater Invisibility - Enhanced stealth
- Death Ward - Death prevention
- Stone Shape - Terrain manipulation

### 5th Tier (5th Level)
**Power Level**: Powerful battlefield control
**Caster Level**: 9th-11th
**Examples**:
- Dominate Person - Mind control
- Scrying - Remote viewing
- Wall of Force - Battlefield division
- Greater Restoration - Major healing
- Legend Lore - Information gathering

### 6th Tier (6th Level)
**Power Level**: Reality-altering effects
**Caster Level**: 11th-13th
**Examples**:
- Heal - Major healing (70 HP)
- True Seeing - True sight
- Word of Recall - Emergency teleport
- Harm - Major damage (14d6)
- Heroes' Feast - Party buff

### 7th Tier (7th Level)
**Power Level**: Planar manipulation
**Caster Level**: 13th-15th
**Examples**:
- Plane Shift - Planar travel
- Project Image - Remote projection
- Teleport - Long-range transport
- Forcecage - Magical imprisonment
- Reverse Gravity - Environmental control

### 8th Tier (8th Level)
**Power Level**: World-changing magic
**Caster Level**: 15th-17th
**Examples**:
- Antimagic Field - Magic suppression
- Power Word Stun - Instant stunning
- Earthquake - Environmental destruction
- Sunburst - Massive light damage
- Mind Blank - Mental protection

### 9th Tier (9th Level)
**Power Level**: Ultimate power
**Caster Level**: 17th-19th
**Examples**:
- Gate - Planar portal/summoning
- True Polymorph - Permanent transformation
- Wish - Reality alteration
- Meteor Swarm - Cataclysmic damage
- Time Stop - Temporal manipulation

### 10th Tier (Epic Spells)
**Power Level**: Campaign-altering
**Caster Level**: 19th-20th (The Great Snorbitzz level)
**Examples**:
- Enhanced Time Stop - Extended temporal control (30 seconds)
- Meteor Swarm (Enhanced) - 20d12 damage
- True Wish - Unrestricted reality alteration
- World Gate - Create permanent planar connections
- Temporal Rewind - Reverse time for limited area

### Player Character Maximum: 40th Level
- The Great Snorbitzz represents the pinnacle of mortal achievement
- Cannot exceed 40th level without becoming divine/immortal
- Access to all spell levels up to 9th tier
- Full access to 10th tier (epic) spells at level 30+
- Limited access to Super-tier magic at level 40

## Spell Power Scaling

### Damage Progression by Tier
- 1st Tier: 2d6-3d6 damage
- 2nd Tier: 4d6-8d6 damage  
- 3rd Tier: 6d6-10d6 damage
- 4th Tier: 8d6-12d6 damage
- 5th Tier: 10d6-14d6 damage

- 8th Tier: 16d6-20d6 damage
- 9th Tier: 18d6-24d6 damage
- 10th Tier: 20d6-30d6 damage

### Save DC Progression
- Base DC: 8 + proficiency + spellcasting ability
- 1st-5th Level: DC 13-15
- 6th-10th Level: DC 16-18
- 11th-15th Level: DC 17-19
- 16th-20th Level: DC 19-21

## Casting Time Requirements

### Standard Casting Times
- Cantrips: 1 action
- 1st-3rd Tier: 1 action
- 4th-6th Tier: 1 action or 1 minute
- 7th-9th Tier: 1 action or 1 hour
- 10th Tier: 1 hour or 8 hours
- Super-tier: 8 hours or special ritual

### The Great Snorbitzz's Casting Advantages
- Can cast 9th tier spells as bonus action (1/day)
- Can ignore material components for spells up to 8th tier
- Can maintain concentration on two spells simultaneously
- Can cast spells through allied creatures as proxy

## Magical Resistance and Immunity

### Sense Person Resistances
Characters who achieve extraordinary levels of power develop special resistances based on their heightened senses and awareness:

#### Level 21-30: Enhanced Senses
- **Advantage** on all Perception checks
- **Resistance** to psychic damage
- **Cannot be surprised** by normal means
- **Advantage** on saves against being charmed or frightened

#### Level 31-35: Preternatural Awareness
- **Truesight** 60 feet
- **Immunity** to psychic damage
- **Advantage** on all Wisdom saves
- **Danger Sense**: Automatically detect hostile intent within 30 feet
- **Cannot be deceived** by illusions of 6th tier or lower

#### Level 36-40: Cosmic Awareness (The Great Snorbitzz Level)
- **Truesight** 120 feet
- **Immunity** to psychic and radiant damage
- **Advantage** on all saving throws
- **True Sight**: See through all illusions and magical effects
- **Cosmic Sense**: Aware of all magical phenomena within 1 mile
- **Temporal Awareness**: Cannot be affected by time manipulation
- **Reality Anchor**: Immune to reality-warping effects
- **Divine Awareness**: Can sense divine presence and power

### Resistance by Tier
- 1st-3rd Tier: Advantage on saves against spells of equal or lower tier
- 4th-6th Tier: Resistance to magical damage from lower tiers
- 7th-9th Tier: Immunity to spells of 3rd tier and below
- 10th Tier: Immunity to all spells except 10th tier and super-tier
- The Great Snorbitzz: Magic absorption (regain spell slots when targeted)

## Character Creation Guidelines

### Maximum Level Progression
1. **Levels 1-5**: Basic spellcasting (up to 3rd tier)
2. **Levels 6-10**: Advanced magic (up to 5th tier)
3. **Levels 11-15**: Master magic (up to 7th tier)
4. **Levels 16-19**: Archmage level (up to 9th tier)
5. **Level 20**: The Great Snorbitzz level (access to 10th tier)

### Multiclassing Considerations
- Each class contributes to overall spell tier access
- The Great Snorbitzz represents single-class mastery
- Multiclass characters may have delayed access to higher tiers

## Campaign Balance Notes

### Using The Great Snorbitzz as Benchmark
- Other 20th level characters should be slightly less powerful
- The Great Snorbitzz's abilities represent absolute maximum potential
- Consider giving other max-level characters 1-2 fewer epic spell slots

### Scaling Encounters
- CR 20+ monsters should have access to 8th-9th tier spells
- World Item holders should be treated as having super-tier capabilities
- The Great Snorbitzz can challenge entire armies single-handedly

## Epic Spell Descriptions

### Enhanced Time Stop (10th Tier)
- **Casting Time**: 1 action
- **Duration**: 30 seconds (vs normal 9 seconds)
- **Effect**: Time stops for everyone except caster
- **Special**: The Great Snorbitzz can bring allies into time stop

### True Wish (10th Tier)
- **Casting Time**: 1 action
- **Effect**: Alter reality without restrictions
- **Cost**: No experience cost for The Great Snorbitzz
- **Limit**: 1 use per long rest

### World Gate (10th Tier)
- **Casting Time**: 8 hours
- **Effect**: Create permanent planar portal
- **Duration**: Permanent
- **Special**: Can connect any two planes

### Temporal Rewind (10th Tier)
- **Casting Time**: 1 minute
- **Effect**: Reverse time in 100ft radius for 1 round
- **Special**: Can undo catastrophic events
- **Limit**: 1 use per week
