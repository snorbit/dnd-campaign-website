# DM Cheat Sheet – Encounters Addendum (Tiered Stat Suggestions)

## Scope
Quick knobs to scale scenes for 5e Tier 1–4. Use with DM_Cheat_Sheet_Encounters.md.

## Targets (by Tier)

### Tier 1 (Lv 1–4)
- **AC**: 12–15 (boss 16)
- **HP per frontliner**: 18–45; boss: 60–90
- **Save DCs**: 12–13 (boss 14)
- **Attack Bonus**: +4 to +5 (boss +6)
- **DPR** (single foe vs one PC): 6–10; boss nova: 14–18 (once)
- **Minions**: 7–10 HP; +3 to hit; 1d6+1 damage

### Tier 2 (Lv 5–10)
- **AC**: 15–17 (boss 18–19)
- **HP per frontliner**: 55–110; boss: 150–220
- **Save DCs**: 14–15 (boss 16–17)
- **Attack Bonus**: +7 to +8 (boss +9)
- **DPR**: 12–22; boss nova: 30–40 (once or lair)
- **Minions**: 18–24 HP; +6 to hit; 1d8+3 damage

### Tier 3 (Lv 11–16)
- **AC**: 17–19 (boss 20–21)
- **HP per frontliner**: 120–200; boss: 300–400
- **Save DCs**: 17–18 (boss 19–20)
- **Attack Bonus**: +9 to +11 (boss +12)
- **DPR**: 22–35; boss nova: 55–75 (mythic phase/lair)
- **Minions**: 35–45 HP; +9 to hit; 2d6+4 damage

### Tier 4 (Lv 17–20)
- **AC**: 19–21 (boss 22–23)
- **HP per frontliner**: 220–320; boss: 500–700
- **Save DCs**: 19–21 (boss 22)
- **Attack Bonus**: +12 to +14 (boss +15)
- **DPR**: 35–55; boss nova: 90–120 (scripted, avoid one‑shots)
- **Minions**: 55–75 HP; +12 to hit; 2d8+6 damage

## Save Profiles
- **Brutes**: High Str/Con saves; weak Int/Wis/Cha.
- **Controllers**: High Int/Wis; weak Str/Dex.
- **Skirmishers**: High Dex; weak Wis.

## Scaling Knobs (fast)
- Add/Subtract minions (2–4) or a lieutenant (elite with +2 AC, +40% HP).
- **Lair Focus**: Enable/disable 1 lair action every other round.
- **Terrain**: Add difficult terrain lines, cover, or hazards that tick with the timer.
- **Timer**: ±1–2 ticks; success can remove a tick; failure adds one.
- **Resources**: Enemy has 1–3 "D" (Disruption) points to counter a spell or tactic; PCs can earn "L" (Leverage) points via objectives.

## Condition Budgets (don't stack too much)
- **Tier 1–2**: 1 hard condition active (stun/paralyze) or 2 soft (restrained/prone/frightened), not both.
- **Tier 3–4**: 1–2 hard or 3 soft, but provide counterplay (saves, action cost removal).

## Sample Packages
- **Audit Skirmish (T2)**: 1 Controller (AC 16, HP 90, DC 15), 2 Bruisers (AC 16, HP 60, +7 to hit 1d10+4), 4 Minions.
- **Choir Assassins (T2–T3)**: 1 Elite (AC 18, HP 160, +10 to hit 2d6+6), 3 Assassins (AC 17, HP 75), smoke + silence zones.
- **Guardian Duel (T3)**: Solo boss (AC 20, HP 360, DC 19), lair focus candles, timer 3.
- **Nazarick Audit Raid (T4)**: Boss (AC 22, HP 600, DC 21), 2 lieutenants, 6 minions, lair action every round until focus disabled.

## Save DC Quick Guide
- **Skill challenge** easy/mod/hard: 12 / 15 / 18 (T1–2), 14 / 17 / 20 (T3–4)
- **Spell save targets**: match tier DCs above; legendary foes have +3–5 on key saves.

## Damage Types & Counters
- **Fire/Necrotic** common for Nazarick; give 1–2 counters (resist/aura) and 1 weakness (radiant, thunder).
- **Anti‑divination static**: impose 25% false positive unless authenticated.

## Rewards & Costs
- Treasure converts to favors, routes, or sanctuaries rather than only gold.
- Each major win: tick a Faction meter; each messy win: add a complication.

## Notes
Use this as a floor/ceiling. Anchor to your party's actual durability and nova potential, and lean on timers and objectives so fights tell the story you want.
