# The Great Tomb of Nazarick: Floor Gazetteer

## Overview
The Great Tomb is not merely a dungeon; it is a layered reality, each floor a self-contained world designed to erode the sanity and resources of invaders. The floors are numbered from surface to depths.

---

## Surface: The Mausoleum
- **Appearance**: A grand, silent cemetery. Crypts line the paths.
- **Guardian**: None (Patrolled by Elder Liches).
- **Hazard**: A permanent *Desecrate* effect. Healing spells heal half as much here.
- **Key Location**: The **Entrance Hall**, a grand foyer where guests are judged.

---

## Floors 1-3: Grave of the Great Dead
- **Theme**: Catacombs / Horror.
- **Floor Guardian**: **Shalltear Bloodfallen**.
- **Description**: Endless, twisting catacombs filled with undead ranging from zombies to Vampire Brides.
- **The Black Capsule**: An interrogation room (specialty of the Torturer).
- **Bridge of Truth**: A narrow bridge over a pit of spikes; illusions hide the real path.

---

## Floor 4: The Underground Lake
- **Theme**: Subterranean Cavern.
- **Floor Guardian**: **Gargantua** (Strategic Siege Golem).
- **Description**: A massive underground ocean. Glowing fungi provide dim light.
- **Features**:
  - The lake is inhabited by aquatic monsters.
  - Gargantua usually slumbers here, looking like a massive rock formation until awakened.

---

## Floor 5: The Great Glacier
- **Theme**: Frozen Wasteland.
- **Floor Guardian**: **Cocytus**.
- **Description**: A world of eternal ice and snow storms. Visibility is effectively zero without magic.
- **Hazard**: **Extreme Cold**. DC 15 Con save every hour or take exhaustion.
- **The Snowball Earth**: A prison made of magical ice that traps victims in suspended animation.

---

## Floor 6: The Jungle
- **Theme**: Primeval Forest / Colosseum.
- **Floor Guardians**: **Aura Bella Fiora & Mare Bello Fiore**.
- **Description**: A massive, artificial sky mimics day and night. A lush jungle surrounds a giant Roman-style colosseum.
- **The Amphitheater**: Where Ainz creates "training scenarios." The VIP box is shielded by a Force Wall.
- **The Village**: A small settlement of Dark Elves (NPCs loyal to the twins).

---

## Floor 7: The Lava River
- **Theme**: Inferno / Hell.
- **Floor Guardian**: **Demiurge**.
- **Description**: Rivers of molten rock, red-iron fortresses, and the screams of the damned.
- **Blazing Temple**: Demiurge's residence. A place of "cultured cruelty."
- **The Farm**: (DM Eyes Only) A specialized area for "resource gathering" (skinning scrolls from regenerators).

---

## Floor 8: The Wilderness
- **Theme**: Desolation.
- **Floor Guardian**: **Victim**.
- **Description**: A barren wasteland where the strongest entities of Nazarick reside. Even the other Guardians hesitate to come here.
- **The Hierarchy**: This floor contains the "Rubedo" unit (unstable, strongest entity) and the Aureole Omega (leader of Pleiades) shrine.
- **Mechanic**: Victim's death triggers a massive debuff (Movement 0, Silence) on all invaders, allowing the Floor 8 elites to slaughter them.

---

## Floor 9: The Royal Suites
- **Theme**: Luxury / Palace.
- **Description**: Marble halls, bathhouses, and the personal quarters of the Guild Members (The Supreme Beings).
- **Features**:
  - **The Round Table**: Meeting room for the 41.
  - **The Spa**: Grants benefits of a Long Rest in 1 hour.
  - **The Bar**: Run by the Sous-Chef. Drinks here grant temporary HP.

---

## Floor 10: The Throne Room
- **Theme**: Divine Majesty.
- **Residency**: **Albedo** (Overseer).
- **Description**: The heart of the dungeon. Contains the **Throne of Kings** (World Item).
- **Ashurbanipal**: The Great Library, guarded by Titus Annaeus Secundus. Contains all spellbooks of the guild.
- **Leduc's Gallery**: The Treasury entrance, requiring a specific ring to enter.
