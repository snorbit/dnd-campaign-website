## Blurb Cards (printable)
- **Salt Stair (◊●)**: Spiral wharf drowned in fog. Tide windows reveal vault doors. Speak names of the drowned to pass.
- **Ashen Lyceum (□)**: Blackened academy. Clockwork librarians still shelve screaming books.
- **Ember Market (●)**: Night bazaar under a burned amphitheater. "One rumor free, one blackmail erased."
- **Drowned Bell (◊)**: A bell tolls under water during storms; revenants answer.
- **Sable Depot (□☼)**: Aid with receipts. Comfort or compliance—not both.
- **Farsung Menhir (□)**: Stone that sings the lattice at dusk. Match the note to retune.
- **Weeping Forge (□)**: Weapons for "merciful" war. Subdue, capture, control.
- **Quiet Orchard (◊)**: No scrying. Veterans and children hide here. Peace has a cost.
- **Echo Vault (◊)**: Relive a hero's memory; pay with one of your own.
