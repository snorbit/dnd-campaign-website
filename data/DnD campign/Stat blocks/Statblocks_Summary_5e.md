Notes
These are encounter concepts and tactical roles. Calibrate numbers to party tier.

Ainz Ooal Gown (Mythic Boss Concept)
- Pillars: Legendary necromancy, battlefield rewrite, resource denial.
- Opener: Time Stop → Debuff/Control setup → Isolation.
- Lair Actions: Gravity well; anti‑divination static; skeletal phalanx rise.
- Mythic Phase: Simulacrum or “Overcast” ritual; radiant countermeasures become precious.
- Counters: Disruption of lair foci; forcing parley with hostages saved.

Albedo (CR 25 template)
- AC: Very high; Legendary Resistances.
- Actions: Black Wing Cleave (line), Guardian Shield Wall (ally cover).
- Reactions: Punish focus-fire; intercept to reduce damage to Ainz.
- Tactics: Duel control; peel the striker; deny advantage stacking.

Demiurge (CR 23 template)
- Control Suite: Fear, charm, walling flames, infernal zones.
- Actions: Hellfire Burst (cone), Domination Charm (save‑or‑flip).
- Legendary: Tactical reposition; hostage switch; call reinforcements on a timer.
- Tactics: Retire on schedule; win at campaign level, not on the mat.

Cocytus (CR 22 template)
- Pillars: Zone denial, forced movement, cold hazards.
- Tactics: Marks a target; rotates stance between offense and fortress.

Shalltear (CR 22 template)
- Pillars: Duelist burst, thrall creation, charm bait.
- Tactics: Split the party; punish lone heroes; parley mid‑fight for drama.

Sebas (CR 21 template)
- Pillars: Single‑target justice, restraint, saves the innocent mid‑combat.
- Tactics: Tests ideals; may throw a fight to make a point.

Lyrin Valehart (Spy‑Mage/Controller)
- Defense: Illusory Escape; Evasion‑like once/short rest.
- Suite: Deception, Insight, Investigation, Detect Thoughts, Silence, Suggestion, Invisibility.
- Special: Auditor’s Mark; network ping complication on 1–2 d6.
- Tactics: Avoid fair fights; create exit routes; trade secrets for positioning.

Regional Effects (Nazarick Orbit)
- Morale Drain: Fear DCs escalate after multiple failures.
- Efficient Undead: Undead ignore minor “turn” effects unless in consecrated ground.
- Static in the Weave: Divination has false‑positive noise unless authenticated with a sigil.
