# Factions of the Realm

## Major Factions

### Re-Founded Empire
- **Goal**: Centralize arcana under panic; draft mages; tighten borders.
- **Hooks**: 
  - Seeks adventurer "consultants"
  - Funds counter-rituals
  - Fears Choir assassinations
- **Influence**: Strong centralized authority, military presence
- **Relations**: 
  - Opposed to Silent Choir
  - Wary of Nazarick's growing power
  - Suspicious of Adventurers' Syndicate
- **Notable Locations**:
  - The Obsidian Spire (Capital)
  - The Arcane Colleges
  - The Border Keeps

### Adventurers' Syndicate
- **Goal**: Keep coin and routes flowing; split over Nazarick patronage.
- **Hooks**:
  - One chapter is Nazarick-funded
  - Smuggling lines double as evacuation corridors
  - Internal power struggles between pro- and anti-Nazarick factions
- **Structure**: Loosely organized guild with autonomous chapters
- **Services**: 
  - Mercenary work
  - Information brokering
  - Safe passage arrangements
- **Notable Chapters**:
  - The Crimson Coin (Neutral)
  - The Black Quill (Pro-Nazarick)
  - The Silver Veil (Anti-Nazarick)

### The Silent Choir
- **Goal**: Purge arcana; assassinate "contaminants"; break the lattice by blood.
- **Hooks**:
  - Offer contraband anti-magic items
  - Target innocent arcanists
  - Radical anti-magic ideology
- **Methods**:
  - Covert operations
  - Assassinations
  - Propaganda against magic users
- **Weaknesses**: 
  - Internal schisms over methods
  - Vulnerable to magical counterintelligence
- **Known Safe Houses**:
  - The Shattered Bell Tavern
  - The Empty Tower
  - The Bleeding Quill Library

### Free Cities Compact
- **Goal**: Collective bargaining for safety; mercantile sanity.
- **Hooks**:
  - Political intrigue and vote-buying
  - Blackmail ledgers
  - Cities that will burn if not saved
- **Structure**: Confederation of independent city-states
- **Economy**:
  - Trade networks
  - Banking and finance
  - Espionage and information trade
- **Member Cities**:
  - Port Veridian (Trade Hub)
  - Highspire (Banking Center)
  - Blackwater (Guild Headquarters)

## Faction Clocks

### Nazarick Influence (0–5)
1. Interest - Minor observations and data gathering
2. Respect - Acknowledges potential value
3. Reliance - Actively seeks cooperation
4. Control - Significant influence over operations
5. Ownership - Complete dominance and integration

### Syndicate Power (0–5)
1. Trust - Basic agreements and information sharing
2. Routes - Established trade and travel networks
3. Leverage - Blackmail and coercion tactics
4. Cartel - Monopoly on key resources
5. State - De facto government control

### Choir Zeal (0–5)
1. Purity - Small, dedicated following
2. Zeal - Active recruitment and propaganda
3. Cells - Organized operations in multiple locations
4. Terror - Open attacks and public executions
5. Purge - Full-scale magical cleansing

### Compact Unity (0–5)
1. Diplomacy - Basic trade agreements
2. Coin - Economic interdependence
3. Intelligence - Shared information networks
4. Sanctions - Coordinated political pressure
5. Hegemony - Unified political entity
