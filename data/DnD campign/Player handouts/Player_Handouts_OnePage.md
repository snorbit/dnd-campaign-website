# Overlord: Ashes of the New World — Player One‑Pager

## Safety & Agreements
- **Lines/Veils** set at Session 0; **X‑Card/Open Door** always on.
- Spotlight rotates; short scenes; check‑ins after tense beats.

## Premise (Plain)
The world is bracing for a planar convergence. Nazarick offers order and survival—at a price. You will choose between comfort with strings and freedom with costs.

## Your Tools

### Parley Tokens (2 per side per scene)
- **Safe Word**
- **Concession**
- **Escalate**
- **Walk Away**

### Faction Meters (0–5)
- **Nazarick**: Interest → Respect → Reliance → Control → Ownership
- **Syndicate**: Trust → Routes → Leverage → Cartel → State
- **Choir**: Purity → Zeal → Cells → Terror → Purge
- **Compact**: Diplomacy → Coin → Intelligence → Sanctions → Hegemony

## Rumors (Hooks)
- The Crown Road "sings" at night—match the note to open hidden doors.
- A ledger counts mercies done by monsters; it weighs more than gold.
- A pale lighthouse forces beasts to negotiate before they bite.

## Map Symbols
- **□** Crown Post (audits)
- **◊** Quiet Mile (no divination)
- **●** Ember Mark (safe rumor-trade)
- **✝** Choir (anti‑magic danger)
- **☼** Nazarick (parley, tribute)

## Early Sites
- **Salt Stair**: Tide windows; brine names; smugglers.
- **Ashen Lyceum**: Clockwork librarians; null‑ink letter.
- **Ember Market**: Wards vs scrying; one rumor free with writ.

## Downtime Picks (choose one)
- **Strengthen a Tie**
- **Seed a Rumor**
- **Risk a Principle**

## Character Prompts
- What principle will you risk first?
- Who would you betray to save thousands?
- What rumor about your past should never be true?

## Card: "Mercy at Scale"
You spare a dangerous foe and turn it into a resource. Mark a Faction meter ±1.
