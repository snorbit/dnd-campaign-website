## Usage Notes for DM
- These handouts can be printed or shared digitally
- Some contain clues, others are red herrings
- Timing of reveals affects campaign pacing
- Physical props enhance immersion (use special paper, wax seals, etc.)
- Some letters may be discovered in different orders based on investigation
