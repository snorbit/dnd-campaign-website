# Super Tier Arsenal: 60 Divine Items & Quests

## Overview
This tome lists **60 Super Tier (Divine/Guild Class)** items. Each item is locked behind a specific **Quest Hook**.
**Distribution Guide**:
- 20 Melee Weapons (Swords, Axes, Spears)
- 20 Ranged/Magic (Bows, Staffs, Grimoires)
- 20 Accessories/Armor (Rings, Amulets, Cloaks)

---

## Section I: Melee Weapons (The Divine Blade)

### 1-5: The Elemental Blades
1.  **Ignis Heart (Greatsword or Longsword)**
    *   **Hook**: "The Volcano's Core." Defeat the Fire Giant Lord in the Magma Flows (Floor 7 sub-zone).
    *   **Power**: Deals extra 4d6 Fire damage. Ignites enemies (1d10/turn).
2.  **Tidal Wave (Trident)**
    *   **Hook**: "The Drowned King." Retrieve from the Leviathan's gut in the Underground Lake (Floor 4).
    *   **Power**: Grants Swim Speed. Casts *Control Water* at will.
3.  **Storm-Ruler (Warhammer)**
    *   **Hook**: "Peak of storms." Climb the loftiest peak during a thunder-event. Kill the Roc guarding it.
    *   **Power**: Deals Thunder damage. Thrown weapon returns to hand (Mjolnir style).
4.  **Gaia's Fist (Maul)**
    *   **Hook**: "The Moving Mountain." Find the Elemental that walks the desert and shatter its core.
    *   **Power**: Creates difficult terrain on impact. Siege weapon (double damage to structures).
5.  **Zephyr's Edge (Rapier)**
    *   **Hook**: "Chasing the Wind." Catch the invisible Air Spirit in the Great Grass Sea.
    *   **Power**: Extra Attack (can attack 3 times instead of 2). Grants *Haste* action once per short rest.

### 6-10: The Slayer Weapons
6.  **Dragon-Bane (Lance)**
    *   **Hook**: "The Last Dragon Lord." Slay a True Dragon Lord (or their avatar).
    *   **Power**: Auto-crit vs Dragons. Resistance to breath weapons.
7.  **God-Slay (Great Axe)**
    *   **Hook**: "The Fallen Angel." Defeat a high-tier Dominion Authority summon.
    *   **Power**: Bypasses all damage resistances. Deals Radiant damage.
8.  **Mage-Eater (Dagger)**
    *   **Hook**: "The Silent Assassin." Kill a Lich in an anti-magic field.
    *   **Power**: On hit, target loses a spell slot. User gains Temp HP.
9.  **Souleater Scythe (Glaive/Scythe)**
    *   **Hook**: "The Reaper's Game." Win a duel against a Death Knight Lord.
    *   **Power**: Kills humanoids under 50HP instantly. Trap their soul (prevents revivify).
10. **King-Maker (Shortsword)**
    *   **Hook**: "The False Throne." Steal the crown jewel of a Human Kingdom.
    *   **Power**: Grants Advantage on Charisma checks. Command nearby allies (Tactician).

### 11-15: The Cosmic Series
11. **Star-Metal Katana**
    *   **Hook**: "Meteor Hunt." Mine the Star-Metal from a fresh crater guarded by aberrations.
    *   **Power**: Cuts through force fields (Wall of Force).
12. **Void-Walker (Greatsword)**
    *   **Hook**: "Into the Abyss." Enter a black hole distortion in the Astral Plane.
    *   **Power**: Deals Force damage. Teleport to handled blade (blink strike).
13. **Moonlight Greatsword**
    *   **Hook**: "Lunar Eclipse." Forge the blade under the light of a blood moon.
    *   **Power**: Fires beams of radiant energy (Ranged melee attack).
14. **Solar Spear**
    *   **Hook**: "Sun's Anvil." Meditate for 3 days in the center of a desert heatwave.
    *   **Power**: Blinds enemies on hit. Casts *Sunbeam*.
15. **Nebula Whip**
    *   **Hook**: "Captured Cloud." Bottle a sentient nebula cloud.
    *   **Power**: Reach 15ft. Grapples enemies on hit with cosmic dust.

### 16-20: The Cursed & Weird
16. **Blood-Drinker (Longsword)**
    *   **Hook**: "The Vampire's kiss." Let Shalltear bite you (survive it) to bless the blade.
    *   **Power**: Heals user for 50% of damage dealt. Curse: User must drink blood daily.
17. **The Severed Arm** (Club/Flail)**
    *   **Hook**: "Gargantua's Scrap." Find a piece of the Siege Golem that fell off.
    *   **Power**: Massive bludgeoning. Knockback 30ft.
18. **Tentacle Rod**
    *   **Hook**: "Deep Ones." Defeat the Kraken Priest in the oceanic trench.
    *   **Power**: Paralyzes targets. 3 attacks per action.
19. **Ghost-Touch Gauntlets**
    *   **Hook**: "Punch a Ghost." Defeat a Banshee Queen with bare hands (using magic coating).
    *   **Power**: Can grapple incorporeal creatures. Force damage unarmed strikes.
20. **Runeforged Bastard Sword**
    *   **Hook**: "Dwarven Lost Art." Recover the Rune-Smith's hammer from the Frost Giants.
    *   **Power**: 5 Rune slots. Customizable damage types (switchable).

## Section II: Ranged & Magic (The Arcane Arsenal)

### 21-25: Staves of Power
21. **Staff of Ainz Ooal Gown (Replica)**
    *   **Hook**: "The Test of Loyalty." Prove absolute loyalty to the Guild (Complete all floor guarding tasks).
    *   **Power**: +3 to Spell Attacks. Summons Primal Elementals.
22. **Staff of the Magi**
    *   **Hook**: "Archmage's Tower." Loot the Obsidian Spire's vault.
    *   **Power**: Spell Absorption. Plane Shift.
23. **Necromancer's Spine**
    *   **Hook**: "Rib of the Lich." Harvest a bone from an Elder Lich Lord.
    *   **Power**: Undead summons have +50 HP and +2 damage.
24. **Verdant Keeper (Druid Staff)**
    *   **Hook**: "Heart of the Forest." Heal the corrupted tree on Floor 6.
    *   **Power**: *Plant Growth* at will. Wild Shape into CR 6 beasts.
25. **Crystalcaster (Sorcerer Focus)**
    *   **Hook**: "Prismatic Dragon." Slay a crystal dragon.
    *   **Power**: Spells can change damage type at will.

### 26-30: Bows & Crossbows
26. **Houyi's Bow (Longbow)**
    *   **Hook**: "Shoot the Sun." Hit a target 600ft away during high noon.
    *   **Power**: Arrows ignite (Radiant). Ignore range penalties.
27. **Perun's Bolt (Heavy Crossbow)**
    *   **Hook**: "Lightning Catcher." Strike a lightning rod during a storm.
    *   **Power**: Lightning damage. Stuns on crit.
28. **Shadow-Whisper (Shortbow)**
    *   **Hook**: "Assassinate the Shadow." Kill a Shadow Demon without being seen.
    *   **Power**: Silent shots. Auto-crit on surprised targets.
29. **Seeking Swarm (Hand Crossbow)**
    *   **Hook**: "Insect Queen." Impress Entoma with a bug offering.
    *   **Power**: Projectiles track targets. Fired bolts become biting insects.
30. **Siege-Breaker (Ballista-Bow)**
    *   **Hook**: "Giant Slayer." Kill a Giant alone.
    *   **Power**: Requires 18 STR. Pin targets to walls (Restrained).

### 31-35: Grimoires & Tomes
31. **Book of the Dead**
    *   **Hook**: "Review the Ledger." Correct a mistake in the Black Scriptorium.
    *   **Power**: Learn 3 Necromancy spells from any list.
32. **Chronicles of Time**
    *   **Hook**: "Time Heist." Steal a book from the past (via temporal dungeon).
    *   **Power**: Re-roll one D20 per fight. *Time Stop* once per day.
33. **Codex of Infinite Planes**
    *   **Hook**: "Dimensional Drifter." Visit 3 different planes in 24 hours.
    *   **Power**: *Gate* spell accuracy 100%. Banishment DC +2.
34. **The Madman's Scrawl**
    *   **Hook**: "Sanity Check." Read the forbidden text in the Ashurbanipal for 1 hour.
    *   **Power**: Psychic Scream spell. User takes 1d4 psychic dmg per use.
35. **Tactician's Manual**
    *   **Hook**: "Chess with Demiurge." Last 10 rounds in a game against Demiurge.
    *   **Power**: Grant allies specific maneuvers (Battle Master light).

### 36-40: Miscellaneous Magic Tools
36. **Orb of Scrying**
    *   **Hook**: "See Everything." Map the entire dungeon Floor 1-3.
    *   **Power**: *Scrying* at will. True Sight 30ft.
37. **Alchemist's Satchel**
    *   **Hook**: "Potion Master." Brew a potion that cures a divine curse.
    *   **Power**: Produce 3 random potions daily.
38. **Bardic Lute of Charms**
    *   **Hook**: "Siren's Song." Out-perform a Siren in the Underground Lake.
    *   **Power**: *Mass Suggestion*. Enemies have Disadvantage on Saves vs Charm.
39. **Summoning Whistle**
    *   **Hook**: "Beast Tamer." Tame a wild Griffon on the peaks.
    *   **Power**: Summons the Griffon mount instantly.
40. **Deck of Many Things (Controlled)**
    *   **Hook**: "Gamble with Luck." Win a game of chance against a Devil.
    *   **Power**: A deck where the bad cards have been removed (mostly).

## Section III: Accessories & Armor (The Divine Guard)

### 41-45: Rings
41. **Ring of Ainz Ooal Gown**
    *   **Hook**: "Guild Membership." Earned only by being accepted as an equal to the Guardians.
    *   **Power**: Teleportation within Nazarick.
42. **Ring of Shooting Stars**
    *   **Hook**: "Wish Upon a Star." Sacrifice a legendary item to the Gacha machine.
    *   **Power**: Cast *Wish* (limited scope) 3 times. Burned after use (or recharges yearly).
43. **Ring of Regeneration**
    *   **Hook**: "Troll Blood." Bathe the ring in the blood of a Troll King.
    *   **Power**: Regain 1d6 HP every turn.
44. **Ring of Spell Storing (Greater)**
    *   **Hook**: "Mana Well." Absorb the blast of a 9th tier spell.
    *   **Power**: Stores up to 9 levels of spells.
45. **Ring of Freedom**
    *   **Hook**: "Unchained." Escape a magical prison (Floor 5's ice prison).

48. **Wisdom of the Owls**
    *   **Hook**: "Library Silence." Read 100 books in the Ashurbanipal in silence.
    *   **Power**: +2 WIS. Advantage on Insight.
49. **Choker of Speed**
    *   **Hook**: "Race the Wind." Beat a Haste-casted monk in a footrace.

50. **Medallion of thoughts**

52. **Mantle of the Beast**

    *   **Power**: STR becomes 25.
58. **Boots of Levitation**
    *   **Hook**: "Walk on Air." Cross a chasm without touching the bridge.
    *   **Power**: Hover speed. Immune to ground hazards.
59. **Gloves of Thievery**
