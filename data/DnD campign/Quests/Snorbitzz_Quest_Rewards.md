
# Snorbitzz Quest Rewards: The Divine Sets

This archive contains the specific Super Tier (Divine Class) equipment sets awarded to party members upon completing the final trials of the Great Snorbitzz.

---

## 1. Troy (Dwarf Fighter)
**Set Name**: *Regalia of the Adamantine Titan*
*A collection of artifacts forged from the core of a dead star, pulsing with the irresistible gravity of the earth itself.*

### **Body: Armor of the Adamantine Titan**
- **Type**: Plate Armor
- **AC**: 26 (Divine Class)
- **Power**:
    - **Unbreakable**: Wearer has resistance to all physical damage (Bludgeoning, Piercing, Slashing) even given by magical weapons.
    - **Titan's Strength**: Wearer's size counts as one size larger for carrying capacity and grappling.
    - **Earthshaker**: As a bonus action, stomp the ground to cast *Earthquake* (centered on self, user immune) once per long rest.

### **Head: Helm of the Mountain King**
- **Type**: Helmet
- **Power**:
    - **Stone Vision**: Grants Tremorsense 60ft and Darkvision 120ft.
    - **Unstoppable**: Immune to Stunned and Petrified conditions.
    - **Crown of the Deep**: Identify any metal or stone origin instantly.

### **Hands: Gauntlets of the Star-Forge**
- **Type**: Gauntlets
- **Power**:
    - **Siege Breaker**: Melee attacks deal double damage to objects and structures.
    - **Magma Grip**: Unarmed strikes deal an extra 2d6 Fire damage. Can heat metal objects by touch (as *Heat Metal* spell/no concentration) at will.

### **Feet: Greaves of Gravity**
- **Type**: Boots/Greaves
- **Power**:
    - **Immovable Object**: You cannot be moved against your will, knocked prone, or pushed while your feet are on the ground.
    - **Meteor Jump**: Your jump distance is tripled. Landing from a jump of 10ft or more causes a localized tremor, knocking adjacent enemies prone (DC 18 STR save).

### **Cloak: Mantle of the Bedrock**
- **Type**: Cloak
- **Power**:
    - **Earthen Camouflage**: While standing still against stone, you are invisible.
    - **Absorb Impact**: The cloak hardens on impact, reducing Critical Hits against you to normal hits.

### **Neck: Amulet of the Core**
- **Type**: Amulet
- **Power**:
    - **Vitality of the Earth**: Constitution score increases by +2 (up to 24).
    - **Regeneration**: While touching natural stone/earth, regain 10 HP at the start of your turn.

### **Ring: Band of the Forge Father**
- **Type**: Ring
- **Power**:
    - **Master Smith**: Proficiency (or Expertise) in Smith's Tools. crafting time is reduced by 90%.
    - **Dwarven Heritage**: Immunity to Poison damage and the Poisoned condition.

### **Held: Hammer of the World-Shaper**
- **Type**: Maul (Artifact)
- **Power**:
    - **Terrain Shift**: As an action, you can strike the ground to create or flatten walls of stone (up to 20ft high/long) at will.
    - **Shatter Reality**: Critical hits deal an extra 4d12 thunder damage and knock the target back 60ft.
    - **Absolute Mass**: This weapon ignores any resistance to Bludgeoning damage.

## 2. Zachery (Undead Paladin)
**Set Name**: *Vestments of the Mourning General*
*Black iron twisted by sorrow and hate. The armor seems to weep blood, and the air around it grows cold with the memory of lost love.*

### **Body: Dread Plate of the Fallen General**
- **Type**: Plate Armor
- **AC**: 25 (Divine Class)
- **Power**:
    - **Love & Hate**: Vulnerability to Radiant damage is removed. Grants Immunity to Necrotic.
    - **Undying Vengeance**: When reduced to 0 HP, you do not fall unconscious. Instead, enter "Vengeance State" for 1 minute (unable to die). If not healed by the end, you die instantly. (1/Dawn).
    - **General's Command**: Allies within 30ft are Immune to Fear/Charm.

### **Head: Visage of the Hollow Man**
- **Type**: Helmet (Full Face)
- **Power**:
    - **Face of Despair**: Enemies who look at the helmet's visor must make a WIS save (DC 18) or be Frightened for 1 minute.
    - **Tears of Blood**: Grants Truesight 30ft (The world looks grey and dead).

### **Hands: Gauntlets of the Iron Grip**
- **Type**: Gauntlets
- **Power**:
    - **Crushing Sorrow**: On a melee hit, the target's speed is reduced by 10ft until the start of your next turn (stacks).
    - **Soul Touch**: You can cast *Bestow Curse* (5th level) through touch 1/day.

### **Feet: Sabatons of the Marching Dead**
- **Type**: Boots
- **Power**:
    - **Relentless Pursuit**: Moving towards a Frightened enemy costs no movement.
    - **Cold Step**: The ground freezes where you walk. You can walk on water (it freezes) and difficult terrain does not slow you.

### **Cloak: Shroud of the Widow**
- **Type**: Cloak
- **Power**:
    - **Aura of Dread**: Enemies within 10ft have Disadvantage on all Saving Throws.
    - **Shadow Form**: When in dim light, attacks against you have Disadvantage.

### **Neck: Locket of the Lost Wife**
- **Type**: Amulet (Engraved "Kurumi")
- **Power**:
    - **Obsessive Bond**: Immunity to Charm spells from anyone other than Kurumi (if she were alive).
    - **Heart of Ice**: Cold Immunity.
    - **Memory of Love**: +2 Charisma (up to 24).

### **Ring: Ring of the Tyrant**
- **Type**: Ring
- **Power**:
    - **Kneel Before Me**: You can cast *Command* (Word: "Kneel") as a Bonus Action at will.
    - **Life Drain**: Once per turn, melee attacks heal you for necrotic damage dealt equal to your Charisma modifier.

### **Held: Sorrow’s Edge (The Mourning Greatsword)**
- **Type**: Greatsword (Artifact)
- **Power**:
    - **Wail of the Widow**: When you hit a creature, all other enemies within 10ft take 1d10 necrotic damage from the sword's psychic scream.
    - **Lover's Sacrifice**: You can spend your own Hit Dice (up to 5) to add an equal number of d10s of Necrotic damage to a strike.
    - **Ghostly Blade**: This weapon can strike into the Ethereal Plane and ignores all AC granted by Armor (natural or manufactured).

## 3. Guts (Human Fighter)
**Set Name**: *Regalia of the Black Swordsman*
*Armor forged in the fires of hell, designed for a warrior who refuses to die, even when the world itself wants him dead.*

### **Body:  Berserker Armor**
- **Type**: Plate Armor (Super Tier)
- **AC**: 20 (But allows DEX bonus up to +5)
- **Power**:
    - **Pain Inhibitor**: You cannot be Charmed or Frightened. You do not fall unconscious at 0 HP. You continue fighting until you take damage equal to your Max HP *beyond* 0.
    - **Beast Within**: As a Bonus Action, activate the armor. You gain Resistance to all damage, +4 STR/DEX, and Extra Attack (3 total). However, you take 1d12 necrotic damage at the end of each turn.
    - **Auto-Repair**: The armor fixes broken bones instantaneously (healing 10 HP start of turn if below half health).

### **Head: Helm of the Beast**
- **Type**: Helmet (Wolf/Beast Shape)
- **Power**:
    - **Bite**: You can make a bite attack as a Bonus Action (1d8 + STR Piercing).
    - **Hunter's Sight**: You can see invisible enemies and into the Ethereal Plane.

### **Hands: The Iron Hand (Prosthetic)**
- **Type**: Gauntlet/Prosthetic Replacement
- **Power**:
    - **Cannon Blast**: Once per short rest, you can fire a cannonball from your hand (10d6 Bludgeoning + 6d6 Fire, 30ft cone).
    - **Magnetic Grip**: You cannot be disarmed.

### **Feet: Greaves of the Struggle**
- **Type**: Boots
- **Power**:
    - **Charge**: If you move at least 20ft straight towards a target, your first attack knocks them Prone (No save if they are Huge or smaller).
    - **Unstoppable**: Difficult terrain costs no extra movement.

### **Cloak: Cape of Shadows**
- **Type**: Cloak
- **Power**:
    - **Whirlwind**: When you crit, the cloak lashes out, dealing 2d6 slashing to all enemies within 5ft.
    - **Deflection**: Reactions to impose Disadvantage on ranged attacks.

### **Neck: Brand of the Sacrifice**
- **Type**: Amulet/Tattoo
- **Power**:
    - **Beacon**: You are aware of any fiend or undead within 1 mile.
    - **Struggle**: When you are critically hit, you gain Advantage on your next attack.

### **Ring: Band of the Eclipse**
- **Type**: Ring
- **Power**:
    - **Inner Demon**: While wearing this ring, you can enter a rage state similar to a Barbarian (3/day), but you do not lose it if you fail to attack or take damage.
    - **Blood Trance**: When you drop below 25% HP, your STR and DEX increases by +2.

### **Held: Dragon Slayer (Transcendent)**
- **Type**: Gargantuan Greatsword (Artifact)
- **Power**:
    - **Too Big to Call a Sword**: Range is 10ft. Deals 3d12 Slashing damage.
    - **Existence Erasure**: On a natural 20, the target must make a DC 18 CON save or be cut out of reality (instant death if CR 10 or lower).
    - **Unyielding Edge**: This weapon cannot be broken or blunted by any means, including magical spells like *Disintegrate*.

## 4. Calilac Magithorne (Wood Elf Druid)
**Set Name**: *Regalia of the Mantis Princess*
*A set of armor grown from the World Tree itself, shaped like the exoskeleton of a divine mantis, shimmering with emerald light.*

### **Body: Carapace of the Mantis Queen**
- **Type**: Druid Armor (Non-Metal, equal to +3 Studded Leather)
- **AC**: 19 (Divine Class) + DEX Modifier
- **Power**:
    - **Chitinous Shell**: Resistance to Piercing and Slashing damage.
    - **Camouflage**: You can cast *Pass Without Trace* at will.
    - **Reaction Spines**: When hit by a melee attack, dealing 2d8 piercing damage to the attacker.

### **Head: Crown of the Insect Hive**
- **Type**: Helmet/Circlet
- **Power**:
    - **Antennae Sense**: Blindsight 30ft. You cannot be surprised.
    - **Hive Mind**: You can communicate telepathically with any insect or beast within 1 mile. They will not attack you unless provoked.

### **Hands: Claws of the Praying Mantis**
- **Type**: Gauntlets/Gloves
- **Power**:
    - **Reaping Strike**: Unarmed strikes deal 1d8 Slashing damage. On a Crit, you sever a limb (if applicable).
    - **Grapnel Vines**: Use a bonus action to shoot a vine 30ft to pull yourself to a location or pull an enemy to you (STR save).

### **Feet: Striders of the Pond**
- **Type**: Boots
- **Power**:
    - **Surface Skimmer**: You can walk on water, quicksand, and spiderwebs without penalty.
    - **Leap of faith**: Your jump distance is quintupled.

### **Cloak: Wings of the Emerald Swarm**
- **Type**: Cloak
- **Power**:
    - **Flight**: As a bonus action, sprout insect wings giving a Fly speed of 60ft for 1 hour (Recharge 5-6).
    - **Swarm Disperse**: When you take damage, you can use your Reaction to dissolve into a swarm of insects, taking 0 damage and teleporting 30ft away. (1/Short Rest).

### **Neck: Amulet of the Lost Kingdom**
- **Type**: Amulet
- **Power**:
    - **Nature's Fury**: +2 Wisdom (up to 24).
    - **Regrowth**: Expend 1 charge to cast *Mass Cure Wounds* (3 charges/dawn).

### **Ring: Ring of the Green Thumb**
- **Type**: Ring
- **Power**:
    - **Instant Growth**: Touch a seed or sapling to make it grow to maturity instantly (can create bridges/cover).
    - **Poison Touch**: Immunity to Poison. Your spells ignore Poison resistance.

### **Held: Staff of the Ancient Hive**
- **Type**: Druid Staff (Artifact)
- **Power**:
    - **Swarm Lord**: You can cast *Insect Plague* (7th level) at will. The swarm follows your mental commands.
    - **Royal Pheromones**: Any Beast or Plant with an Intelligence of 8 or lower must succeed on a DC 20 Wisdom save or be charmed by you permanently.
    - **Nature’s Bastion**: While holding the staff, you have a +3 bonus to your AC.

## 5. Wimbie (Rock Gnome Cursificer)
**Set Name**: *Regalia of the Crimson Auditor*
*A set of reality-warping gear that treats the world as a buggy simulation to be corrected... or deleted. It pulses with a flickering red light and emits a faint, high-pitched data hum.*

### **Body: Chassis of the Malignant Core**
- **Type**: Cursificer Armor (Composite Metal/Crystal)
- **AC**: 22 (Divine Class) + Int Modifier (Max +5)
- **Power**:
    - **Logic Shield**: You have Advantage on all saving throws against spells and other magical effects.
    - **War Crime Protocol**: When an ally within 60ft casts a spell of 1st level or higher, you can use your Reaction to immediately cast the same spell (at its lowest level) without consuming a spell slot.
    - **Data Overflow**: You are immune to being Stunned or Paralyzed.

### **Head: Monocle of the Tactical God**
- **Type**: Headwear/Glass
- **Power**:
    - **Optimization Sight**: You gain Truesight out to 60 feet. You can see through solid objects up to 5 feet thick.
    - **Weakness Analysis**: As a bonus action, analyze one creature you can see. For the next minute, you and your allies deal an extra 2d10 damage of a type the target is vulnerable to (or force damage if none) on all hits.
    - **Genius Level**: Your Intelligence score increases by +4 (Maximum of 30).

### **Hands: Saboteur’s Digital Clutches**
- **Type**: Gauntlets
- **Power**:
    - **Remote Hijack**: You can use an action to take control of any non-sentient mechanical object or construct within 60ft (DC 18 Int Save for constructs).
    - **Cursed Injection**: When you hit with a melee attack or spell, you can apply a "Glitch Curse" (Target has Disadvantage on all rolls for 1 round).

### **Feet: Kinetic Displacement Striders**
- **Type**: Boots
- **Power**:
    - **Initiative God**: Your Initiative bonus is doubled. You can never be surprised.
    - **Frame-Skip**: As a bonus action, you can teleport up to 30 feet to an unoccupied space you can see. This does not provoke opportunity attacks.

### **Cloak: Mantle of the Glitched Reality**
- **Type**: Cloak
- **Power**:
    - **Displacement**: You appear to be standing a few inches from your actual location. Attacks against you have Disadvantage unless the attacker has Truesight.
    - **Invisible to the Machine**: Mechanical beings, constructs, and undead with an Intelligence of 6 or lower cannot see you unless you attack them.

### **Neck: Amulet of the Broken Algorithm**
- **Type**: Amulet
- **Power**:
    - **Probability Hack**: Whenever you roll damage for a spell or ability, you can reroll any 1s or 2s. You must use the new roll.
    - **Error Correction**: Once per short rest, you can turn a failed saving throw into a success.

### **Ring: Band of the Forbidden Script**
- **Type**: Ring
- **Power**:
    - **Silent Execution**: You can cast *Silence* and *Bestow Curse* (5th level) at will, requiring no components.
    - **Delete Command**: Once per long rest, you can target one creature with less than 100 HP. It must succeed on a DC 20 Constitution save or be instantly deleted (erased from existence).

### **Held: The Universal Key (Source Code Focus)**
- **Type**: Multi-tool/Scepter (Artifact)
- **Power**:
    - **Rewrite Object**: You can spend 1 minute to change the properties of a non-living object (e.g., turn a wooden door into paper, or a steel lock into water).
    - **Admin Override**: You can cast *Counterspell* at 9th level once per short rest without consuming a spell slot.
    - **The Gacha Box**: Once per dawn, you can pull a random common or uncommon magic item from the device. It lasts for 24 hours before vanishing.

## 6. Sam (High Elf Wizard)
**Set Name**: *Regalia of the Temporal Weaver*
*Garments woven from the threads of time itself, glowing with a faint, blue aura that makes the wearer seem to flicker in and out of the present.*

### **Body: Robe of the Timeless Archmage**
- **Type**: Robe (Super Tier)
- **AC**: 15 + Int Modifier
- **Power**:
    - **Temporal Shielding**: You have Advantage on all saving throws against spells.
    - **Archmage's Efficiency**: Whenever you cast a spell of 1st level or higher, you can roll a d20. On a 19 or 20, the spell slot is not consumed.
    - **Time-Lost Presence**: Ranged attacks against you have Disadvantage as your position vibrates between seconds.

### **Head: Circlet of Chronos**
- **Type**: Headwear/Circlet
- **Power**:
    - **Precognition**: Your Initiative bonus increases by your Intelligence modifier (Stacks with existing +16).
    - **Focused Mind**: You cannot lose concentration on a spell due to taking damage.
    - **Intellect of the Ages**: Your Intelligence score increases by +4 (Maximum of 30).

### **Hands: Gauntlets of the Glimpse**
- **Type**: Gauntlets
- **Power**:
    - **Accelerated Casting**: Once per short rest, you can cast a spell with a casting time of 1 Action as a Bonus Action instead.
    - **Rewind Strike**: When you miss with a spell attack, you can use your Reaction to reroll the attack. You must use the new result.

### **Feet: Boots of the Rift-Walker**
- **Type**: Boots
- **Power**:
    - **Step Between Seconds**: You can cast *Misty Step* at will without consuming a spell slot.
    - **Anchor of Time**: You are immune to any effect that would teleport you against your will.

### **Cloak: Mantle of the Distant thread**
- **Type**: Cloak
- **Power**:
    - **Echo Fade**: When you take damage, you can use your Reaction to become semi-transparent until the end of your next turn. During this time, you have Resistance to all damage. (1/Short Rest).
    - **Haste Aura**: Allies within 10ft of you have their movement speed increased by 10ft.

### **Neck: Pendant of the Eternal Second**
- **Type**: Amulet
- **Power**:
    - **Time Stop (Minor)**: Once per long rest, you can cast *Time Stop*, but it only lasts for 1 round.
    - **Aeon Senses**: You have Truesight out to 120ft.

### **Ring: Ring of the Chronomancer**
- **Type**: Ring
- **Power**:
    - **Recall Mana**: As a bonus action, you can regain one expended spell slot of 5th level or lower. (1/Dawn).
    - **Stabilize Reality**: You and allies within 30ft are immune to the *Slow* spell and similar temporal slowing effects.

### **Held: Chronicles of Time**
- **Type**: Grimoire (Artifact)
- **Power**:
    - **Fate Alteration**: You have 3 Luck points (as per the *Lucky* feat) that recharge every dawn.
    - **Master of Time**: You can cast *Slow* and *Haste* at will, requiring no components.
    - **Temporal Archive**: You can spend 1 minute reading the book to ask the DM one question about the past or future of your current location with a 50% chance of going insane (as per *Contact Other Plane* but with 100% accuracy ).
